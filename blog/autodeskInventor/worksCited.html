Getting Started

A brief summary of Chapter 1: Getting Started from Parametric Modeling with Autodesk Inventor 2020 .

Read on for more background and details.

  • CAD programs began in the 1950s, and progressed from 2D programs to 3D programs as computer hardware improved.
  • 3D wireframe modeling was a good progression, but due to the lack of surface definitions ambiguity was an issue.
  • Constructive solid geometry (CSG) is the method of subtracting or adding primative shapes.
  • Boundary representation (B-rep) is the method of sweeping or rotating points,edges, and surfaces in a third dimension to create a multitude of surfaces, which are unioned to create a complete 3D shape.
  • "...parametric means that the geometric definitions of the design, such as dimensions, can be varied at any time during the design process."1
